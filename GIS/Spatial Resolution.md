# Spatial Resolution
The smallest angular distance between two objects that can be resolved by the RS sytem.
Basically, how much distance is encoded by the pixels?
![[Pasted image 20220110102914.png]]
Generally pixel sizes range from:
- 10m
- 20m
- 30m
- 36.5m
- 79m
- 1km!!!!!! which is huge, mostly used for weather
